SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST 
1: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money .PINs are only required when you are sending money
2: Always verify the receiver 's UPI ID and name on your screen before hitting send, especially when paying unfamiliar people
3. Use strong, unique passwords for all your banking, student portal  and social media accounts
4.Enable Two-Factor Authentication on your email and other  communication apps.
5.Do not click on suspicious links in SMS or WhatsApp messages which claims your account is blocked 
6. Avoid making sensitive or  financial transactions which contains private information  while connected to public or campus Wi-Fi networks
7. Regularly check your bank statements for any unauthorized or small test deductions.
8. Official Reporting Channels: If you suspect cyber fraud immediately report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call the 24x7 Helpline which is 1930

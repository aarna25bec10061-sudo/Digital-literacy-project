 5 Do's - 
1.Think twice before posting content publicly especially if it contains your private information
2.maintain a professional tone when you interact with professionals
3.Respect the privacy of others by asking before tagging them to anything
4.Use privacy settings to protect personal data
5.Verify the accuracy of information before sharing news to other people

5 Don'ts -

do not post offensive, discriminatory, or disrespectful comments
Do not share your login credentials or personal details
Do not engage in online arguments 
do not spread rumors or misinformation
do not post someone else without their permission
